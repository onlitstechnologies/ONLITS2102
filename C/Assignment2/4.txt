Algorithm: To accept a year and find whether it is a leap year or 
			a common year
Input: year
Ouput:	leap or common

1. 	Start
2. 	Read year
3. 	If(year mod 4 = 0) then
		if(year mod 100 <> 0)
			Print "Leap Year"
		Else
			Print "Commong Year"
	Else
		Print "Common Year"
	(End If)
	
	
4	If(year mod 400 == 0)
		Print ("Leap Year")
	Else
		Print("Common Year")
5.	Stop
